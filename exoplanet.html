Types of Exoplanets

  • Hot Jupiter A gas giant that orbits very close to its star
  • Super-Earths A type of exoplanet with a mass between that of Earth and Neptune
  • Neptune-like Exoplanets that have similar characteristics to Neptune
  • Gas Giants Large planets composed mainly of gas
  • Rogue Planets Planets that do not orbit any star
  • Carbon Planets Planets primarily composed of carbon
  • Terrestrial Planets Rocky planets similar to Earth
